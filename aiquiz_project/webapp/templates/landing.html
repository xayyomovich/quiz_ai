FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is CLD really free to use?

Yes! CLD is completely free for both teachers and students. Create unlimited tests and assess unlimited students at no cost.

How does the AI test generation work?

Simply describe the topic, difficulty level, and number of questions you need. Our AI will generate a complete test with multiple-choice questions in 5-10 seconds.

What is the anti-cheat system?

Our system monitors student behavior during tests, detecting tab switching, copy-paste attempts, and other suspicious activities. If cheating is detected, the test is automatically submitted with only answered questions graded.

Can I create tests manually?

Absolutely! You can paste your own questions and answer keys, or use our AI to generate them. Both options are available.

Do students need to create accounts?

Yes, students need a free account to take tests. This allows them to track their progress and view past results.
